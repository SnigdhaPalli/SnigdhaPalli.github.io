Doggos!

Dogs are incredibly important to me. Just seeing pictures of them give me so much happiness and help my mental health greatly. Though, I have never had one, I dream of being a dog mom one day. Dogs are so special and have so much love to give. Unfortunately, with classes, work, and an internship, I do not have the time to give a dog the attention that it needs, so I can not get one yet.

Here are my favorite breeds in no particular order:

  • Pitbulls (aka Pibbles)
  • Siberian Huskies (aka Moon Moon Floofs)
  • Golden Retrievers (aka goldy bois)
  • I give up. I love them all too much to pick favorites

Fun Fact: When I was a child, my mom told me she was deathly allergic to dogs to get me to stop asking for one. She was not.

Horses!

I started riding horses when I was 4 years old and they have since always been a part of my life. During high school, when I got an opportunity to do some independent research, so I decided to research equine-assisted psychotherapy (EAP). I started interning/working at a therapuetic riding facility, LeCheval Stable, and found that I liked teaching horse back riding and training horses much more than I liked competing.

Some of my responsibilities as an instructor include:

  1. Help riders tack and groom horses
  2. Be available to side-walk or lead the horse
  3. Help riders prepare for the Special Olympics
  4. Muck stalls and feed horses
  5. Make sure everyone is safe while having a good time!
Visit our website for more information
